What Is The UFC?

The UFC is a Mixed Martial Arts Organization that was founded on November 12, 1993, in Denver, Colorado. Its original purpose was for martial artists of various disciplines like Karate, Boxing, Kickboxing, Muay Thai, Judo, and others to gather together and see which of their martial arts were the most effective with minimal rules and no weight classes.

What Is The UFC Like Now?

In 2001, Dana White became the CEO of the UFC after his childhood friends took it over. He effectively is mostly responsible for the changes that have been made to the UFC. In this time, the UFC doesno single night tournaments, or lack of weight classes. it was during Dana White's era that fighters had starte to truly mix up the martial arts.

What is Mixed Martial Arts?

Although at the conception of Mixed Martial Arts, it was only a ruleset, now it is a fullblown art and combat sport of its own. It combines various techniques from other martial arts disciplines like: striking from Boxing, Muay Thai, Karate, Kickboxing,etc , grappling from Sambo, Brazilian Jiu-Jitsu, Judo, etc. The fighters fight in an octogon in contrast to boxing in a ring where they use striking, takedowns, submissions to either win by decision or by knockout.
